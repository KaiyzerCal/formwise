# 🎥 Video Upload & AI Form Analysis — Quick Start

## What's New?
Upload your workout videos and get instant AI-powered form feedback with visual pose detection overlays.

## Where to Find It
**Main Menu → Technique Compare → Reference Tab → Custom Video**

## How to Use in 5 Steps

### 1️⃣ Upload Your Workout Video
- Click **"Upload reference video"** or **"Upload your video"**
- Select a video file from your device (MP4, MOV, WebM, etc.)
- Video appears in preview player

### 2️⃣ Enable AI Analysis
- Click the **⚡ AI** button (lightning icon)
- CustomVideoAnalyzer panel appears below the upload button

### 3️⃣ Analyze Your Form
- Click **"Analyze Form"** button
- Wait 15-30 seconds for AI analysis to complete
- Loading spinner shows progress

### 4️⃣ Review Results
- **Form Score (0-100)** appears with color-coded rating
  - 🟢 80+: Excellent form
  - 🟡 60-79: Good with improvements needed
  - 🔴 Below 60: Significant corrections needed
- See **Strengths** ✓
- Review **Critical Errors** ✗
- Read **Action Items** (numbered fixes)
- Check **Next Steps** for progression guidance

### 5️⃣ Compare & Learn
- Video plays in left panel with **pose skeleton overlay**
- Reference video/exercise shows in right panel
- Toggle **Overlay** to see body detection on both videos
- Adjust playback **Speed** (0.25x - 2x) to study movements
- Use **Guides** and **Alignment** toggles for visual reference

## Key Features

### Visual Overlays
- **Skeleton Pose**: MediaPipe pose detection shows body joints and connections
- **Joint Labels**: Toggle to see joint names (wrist, knee, hip, etc.)
- **Guides**: Grid lines for positional reference
- **Alignment**: Reference lines for proper positioning

### Comparison Modes
| Mode | View | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **Side-by-Side** | 50/50 split | Comparing two videos |
| **Overlay** | Full-screen + corner PiP | Detailed form analysis |

### Analysis Sections
| Section | What It Shows | How to Use |
|---------|---------------|-----------|
| **Form Score** | 0-100 rating | Quick overall assessment |
| **Strengths** | What you do well | Build confidence |
| **Critical Errors** | Injury-risk mistakes | Priority fixes |
| **Action Items** | Specific how-to fixes | Improve form |
| **Next Steps** | Weight/difficulty advice | Progress training |

## Tips for Best Results

✅ **Good Lighting** — Bright, even lighting helps pose detection
✅ **Full Body in Frame** — Try to include entire body when possible
✅ **Clear Angle** — Film from 45-90° angle (side view works best)
✅ **Normal Speed** — Film at regular pace, not slow motion
✅ **Multiple Videos** — Upload from different angles for comprehensive feedback

❌ **Avoid** — Dark lighting, back-to-camera, cropped videos, extreme angles

## Speed Controls
- **Play/Pause**: Space bar or button
- **Speed**: 0.25x, 0.5x, 1x, 2x buttons
- **Seek**: Click slider or arrow keys to jump frames

## Keyboard Shortcuts
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **Space** | Play/Pause |
| **←** | Step backward |
| **→** | Step forward |

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Analysis failed" | Try different video or improve lighting |
| No pose skeleton | Enable "Overlay" toggle |
| Audio not playing | Video is muted (works as intended) |
| Slow analysis | Analysis is real-time, wait 20-30 sec |
| Can't upload file | Check file format (MP4, MOV, WebM) |

## What Gets Analyzed

The AI coaches you on:
- ✅ Body alignment and posture
- ✅ Joint positioning and angles
- ✅ Movement quality and control
- ✅ Balance and stability
- ✅ Range of motion
- ✅ Muscle engagement cues
- ✅ Common form faults for that exercise

## Example: Back Squat Analysis

**Your Video** → **Upload & Analyze** → **Get Feedback:**

```
Form Score: 72/100 (Good with improvements)

✓ Strengths:
  • Good depth, reaching parallel
  • Stable knee position
  • Controlled descent

✗ Critical Errors:
  • Torso leans too far forward
  • Heels slightly lifting

Action Items:
  1. Cue: "Chest up, shoulders back"
  2. Practice: Box squats to teach depth
  3. Strengthen: Anterior core work

Next Step: Add weight when knees stop caving
```

## Integration with Technique Compare

- **Left Panel**: Your uploaded video with pose overlay
- **Right Panel**: Reference video OR library skeleton animation
- **Sidebar**: AI analysis results (when enabled)
- **Playback**: Synchronized across both videos

## File Size Limits

- Maximum: Typically 500MB+ depending on browser
- Recommended: 50-200MB for fast upload
- Format: MP4, MOV, WebM, Ogg (any HTML5 video)

## Privacy & Data

- Videos are uploaded temporarily for analysis only
- Original files stay on your device (local storage)
- Analysis is provided by AI vision model
- Results are displayed locally in your browser
